Tennis Sports

Sport Overview
Tennis, also known as Lawn Tennis , is a popular team sport played on a field. It involves two players trying to score point by playing with a ball into the opponent's court .

Rules and Regulations
Tennis has specific rules governing gameplay, such as ace, advantage, and service. Understanding these rules is essential for fair and competitive play.

Equipment and Gear
Tennis players typically wear bands and jerseys during matches. The required equipment is the ball,racket making it accessible to players of all ages.

Playing Field
Tennis is played on a field with opponents at each end. The field is marked with lines for various purposes, such as the service line and end line.

Famous Players
Tennis has produced many legendary players known for their skills and achievements in the sport. Some of the greatest Tennis players in history include Roger Federer, Nadal , Serena Williams.
